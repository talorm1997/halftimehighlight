Halftime Highlight

About our website


Our website's mission is to inform people about the basics and the highlights of the Superbowl halftime show that takes place once a year, and drives people all over the world CRAZY.

The website shows the best concerts (in our opinion), the worst - which not many know about, and the history of how it all started - from being a pee brake acompanied by some music - to one of the most attractive and exclusive shows in the world, which millions of people expect and watch every year as much as the game itself - and even more....


The Website developed by Sophie Asaph and Talor Mishani, as the final project of "website development" course, first year (2023).
